Pursuit

Network. Connect. Experience


Inspiration
The job seeking process for college students can be very difficult. Many students find it challenging because they feel they do not have enough job/internship experience, nor do they know what specific career field they want to pursue. There are also many professional degrees that require job shadowing before moving on to graduate school or entering the field.

What is Pursuit?
Pursuit is an application designed to help people connect with companies for the purpose of setting up a job shadowing experience. Pursuit accomodates two types of users; Companies and Applicants. Companies can create profiles where they can post openings for jobs they have available for shadowing. Applicant users can create profiles that serve as a sort of resume and will have the ability to search for jobs/companies that are offering shadowing opportunities. On their profile they will also be able to specify certain locations and/or jobs they are interested in and be alerted when an opportunity arises that matches their criteria.
